Overt Coffee, My Favorite!

Tiny, But Wonderful
Nestled in the West Wash Park neighborhood, Overt is a unique cafe with a quaint patio where one can enjoy the perfect cup of coffee.
Huckleberry
One of the other reasons I love Overt, other than the cute atmosphere and offer delicious coffee and pastries, is that they support and sell coffee from my favorite local roastery Huckleberry.
